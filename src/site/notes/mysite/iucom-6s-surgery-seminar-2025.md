##### **3. Common Pitfalls to Avoid**

- **3. Misinterpreting early normal labs**

##### **Short Summary**

Modern appendicitis management relies on clinical scores, CT imaging, and early laparoscopic appendectomy. Antibiotics-only treatment is an option in selected uncomplicated cases but carries recurrence risk. Common pitfalls include misdiagnosis in atypical cases, ignoring normal early labs, and overtreating abscesses with immediate surgery. The best outcomes arise from structured, evidence-based diagnostic and treatment pathways.
