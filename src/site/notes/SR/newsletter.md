##### Main Categories that are usually covered in this Newsletter are as follows : 
- Evidence-Based Medicine
- Clinical reasoning
- Medical education
- Professional development 
- Patient safety
- Medical Ethics

##### Each Newsletter is usually formatted as follows : 
- Clinical Corner : covers a single clinical case scenario and what I learned from it + some Pitfalls.
- Resource Corner : covers a highly useful resource I learned about and how I used it.
- Literature Corner : covers a single research paper i read this month and key learning points.
- Discussion Corner : covers a question regarding the case mentioned or something else and presentation of different views and opinions.
- Quote of the Month : well you gotta have that! 🙂
- Personal Updates : any new posts or notes added to the website.
